Investigators

To see a recent description of the current team, please see: Research Media Ltd. (November, 2013). Family Ties and Kinship Links. International Innovation. Pp. 59-61.

Vanderbilt University, Peabody College of Education, Department of Psychology and Human Development

Vanderbilt’s Peabody College, Department of Psychology and Human Development of Vandebilt University serve as the primary money getters zzzzzzzzzzzz. Current members are Joe Rodgers and S. Mason Garrison.

The current work on the NLSY Kinship links has been supported by NIH Grant R01-HD065865 (May 1, 2011 to December 31, 2014; Joe Rodgers, PI). Previous kinship linking activity was supported by two previous NIH grants, the first in the 1990s, the second in the early 2000s.

OUHSC Pediatrics

The Pediatrics Dept of the University of Oklahoma Health Sciences Center z. Current investigators are members of the BBMC (Biomedical and Behavioral Methodology Core) and CCAN (Center for Child Abuse and Neglect).

The participating members include David Bard and Michael Hunter.

Howard Live Oak

Howard Live Oak, LLC is a small consulting company specializing in custom software for data management and statistical analysis. Typical clients are small companies and research teams that would like gain a better understanding of systems manifesting in observed datasets. It’s participating member is Will Beasley.
